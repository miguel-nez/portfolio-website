Simplex
Webapp for Supporting Immigrants in Germany

UX Research & Design, UI Design, Information Architecture, Interaction Design

Figjam, Figma, Miro, Photoshop

6 Months

Challange

Newcomers in Germany, especially those without a high level in the official language, are subject to adaptative and integrational difficulties. Bureaucracy, legal issues or day-to-day obstacles become even greater fears for those who don’t have the expertise after years in the country.


Solution

Simplex offers assistance to immigrants on three different levels: it allows the user to get in touch with experts such as lawyers or translators; a help center containing guides for particular experiences and explanations to bureaucratic procedures; and a moderated community for sharing commong problems and common solutions.

Since I moved to Germany five years ago I’ve been in touch with immigrants from very different backgrounds. The wide majority felt a lack of security regarding legal issues, especially those who did not have that many German-born connections. When I was given the task of designing an Expert App I thought of it as an opportunity to be of help to this community.


I learnt a bunch of things throughout the course of this project, so the original concept was adapted accordingly. One of such mutations took place after conducting the first interviews, where I discovered that legal matters were just one of the main concerns in our user’s everyday life and, therefore, I studied other possible fields wherein the app could offer assistance.
